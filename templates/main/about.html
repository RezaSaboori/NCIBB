About NCIBB

NCIBB (National Center for Integrative Bioinformatics and Biotechnology) is a leading research institution dedicated to advancing the field of computational biology and bioinformatics.

Our Mission

To provide cutting-edge bioinformatics solutions, foster innovation in computational biology, and support researchers worldwide in their quest to understand complex biological systems through data-driven approaches.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in integrative bioinformatics, bridging the gap between biological data and meaningful scientific insights through advanced computational methods and collaborative research.

Core Values
